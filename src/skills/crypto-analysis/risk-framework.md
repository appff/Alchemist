# Crypto Risk Assessment Framework

Standardized criteria for evaluating cryptocurrency investment risk across five dimensions.

## Risk Matrix

| Risk Factor | Low | Medium | High |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Market Volatility** | Beta < 1.0 vs BTC | Beta 1.0–1.5 vs BTC | Beta > 1.5 vs BTC |
| **FDV/MCap Ratio** | < 1.5x | 1.5–3x | > 3x |
| **Liquidity** (24h Vol / MCap) | > 10% | 5–10% | < 5% |
| **Token Concentration** | Top 10 holders < 30% | 30–60% | > 60% |
| **Protocol TVL Trend** (30d) | Growing > 10% | Stable ±10% | Declining > 10% |

## Market Risk Assessment

- **Low**: Large-cap (top 20), high liquidity, established track record (>3 years)
- **Medium**: Mid-cap (top 20–100), moderate liquidity, 1–3 year track record
- **High**: Small-cap (100+), low liquidity, less than 1 year track record

## Tokenomics Risk Assessment

- **Low**: Fixed or deflationary supply, >80% circulating, no upcoming cliff unlocks
- **Medium**: Moderate inflation (5–15% annual), 50–80% circulating, gradual vesting
- **High**: High inflation (>15% annual), <50% circulating, large cliff unlocks within 12 months

## Protocol Risk Assessment (DeFi)

- **Low**: Multiple audits, battle-tested (>2 years), decentralized governance, multi-chain
- **Medium**: Audited, 1–2 years live, semi-decentralized governance
- **High**: New/unaudited, single chain, centralized admin keys, complex composability

## Regulatory Risk Assessment

- **Low**: Commodity classification likely (BTC, ETH post-Merge), clear regulatory status
- **Medium**: Utility token, some regulatory uncertainty, compliant team
- **High**: Potential security classification, anonymous team, regulatory action history

## Liquidity Risk Assessment

- **Low**: Listed on 5+ major exchanges, deep order books, high CEX + DEX volume
- **Medium**: Listed on 2–4 major exchanges, moderate depth
- **High**: 1 exchange or DEX-only, thin order books, high slippage on $100K+ trades
